Mwinga Mwanjala (800m & 1500m)

Born and raised in The United Republic of Tanzania, Mwinga Mwanjala began her track and field career at the age of 8. She started dominating the inter-school competition and rose up to the national level. She went on setting the Tanzanian record of 4 minutes 34.6 seconds in the 1500 meters then went up to representing the nation in the international competitions. At the age of 14, she went on representing Tanzania in Millrose Games at Madison Square Garden indoor competition as the only female athlete on her team. .
